ABOUT US

PhASLA (Physics and Solution Learning Assistant) is a website with free-to-access worksheets for mathematical physics for Mapúa University students taking the General Physics 1 course.

Currently, our worksheets were sourced from publicly available databases that were then transcribed into our website. Eventually, we aim to implement an algorithm that will provide unique, accurate, and quality worksheets with these worksheets as a basis.

With it, we hope to expand to the larger field of mathematical topics outside this course.
